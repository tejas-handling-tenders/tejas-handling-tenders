Frequently Asked Questions

What is the success rate for tenders?

We have 75%+ success rate across 200+ tenders handled. Success depends on product quality, pricing strategy, and compliance which we optimize for maximum wins.

Do I need MSME registration for government tenders?

Yes, MSME/Udyam registration is mandatory for most government tenders and provides benefits like price preference and exemptions.

How long does GeM registration take?

GeM seller registration takes 3-5 working days including document verification and profile setup.

Do you guarantee tender wins?

No guarantee as tenders are competitive, but we maximize your chances through perfect compliance, competitive pricing, and proven strategies.
